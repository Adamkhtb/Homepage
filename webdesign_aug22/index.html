This is a webpage intended to explain a little bit about Magic the Gathering

Created in 1993, MAGIC: THE GATHERING is the world's first trading card game. Players take turns battling one another by casting spells, summoning creatures and using artifacts depicted on individual cards drawn from their personalized decks. Highly strategic gameplay that is customizable to any individual's playstyle" - Hasbro.com

There are over 20 different formats that exist but the most popular are...

  • Commander
  • Standard
  • Limited
  • Modern

There is even a way to play online that can be downloaded on the official website

The cards used in the tabletop game feature work and styles from a wide variety of artists.
